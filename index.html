I consume everything. I retain nothing. And I was tired of it.

I used to listen to Diary of a CEO, Jay Shetty, and Codie Sanchez every week. I'd feel inspired, grab my phone, frantically type notes — and then never look at them again. My notes app became a graveyard.

I'd highlight pages in books I never returned to. Write down ideas that disappeared into folders. Consume more and more while changing less and less.

The problem wasn't that I wasn't trying. The problem was that no tool was built to help me actually use what I learned.

So I built Retain — a personal knowledge companion that makes knowledge stick.

How it works

Four steps that actually
make knowledge land

Capture — zero friction

Speak your reaction while listening. Snap a photo of your book highlights. One tap. No pausing, no typing, no disruption.

Organise — automatically

AI reads your notes and tags them by topic. Mindset. Business. Health. No manual sorting. No folders to maintain.

Resurface — at the right time

Every morning, one insight from your library. Timed so your brain doesn't forget it. Spaced repetition, made beautiful.

Implement — actually do it

"What will you do with this?" A small prompt. A small commitment. The step everyone skips and the one that changes everything.

✦ Unique Feature

Your physical book highlights, finally digital

You highlight books. You never return to them. Retain changes that with one photo.

Open Retain, point your camera at any highlighted page, and the AI reads only your highlighted text — ignoring everything else — and adds it to your personal library alongside your podcast notes.
